Karl Kemble - Class of 1960






Earned 7 Varsity Letters in Football and Wrestling at Hershey
Was 3 time All South Penn All Star in Football
Voted Outstanding Player in Football in 1959 by his teammates
Was named to the AP's All Pennsylvania Football Team, being a Third Team selection
AP's All State Team Honorable Mention in Football his Senior year
Won 3 consecutive District 3 Wrestling titles
Won 1 Regional Wrestling title
Finished runner-up at PIAA State Championships in Wrestling his Freshman year
Named to the District III Wrestling Hall of Fame in 1982
Played Football and Wrestled at Shippensburg University
2 Year Captain of the Wrestling Team at Shippensburg University
Lost only 3 wrestling matches in 3 years of Wrestling at Shippensburg University.
2 time winner of the Charles Eurich Outstanding Wrestler Award at Shippensburg University







Rebekkah May Manney - Class of 1998







Earned 10 Varsity letters in Field Hockey, Basketball and Soccer
2-time MPC All Star 2nd team selection in Soccer
MPC All Star in Field Hockey in 1997
Team Captain in Field Hockey in 1997
Patriot News Big 11 honorable mention in Field Hockey in 1997
2-time MPC All Star in Basketball
Patriot News Big 15 in Basketball in 1998
Team Captain in Basketball in 1998
Career 1,000 point scorer in Basketball
Earned 4 varsity letters in Basketball at Bucknell University
2001 winner of the Bison Award for Excellence on and off the court at Bucknell University
Patriot League Champions in 2002 in Basketball
Made NCAA playoffs for Basketball in 2002
Team Captain in Basketball her senior year







Katie Stephens Weaver - Class of 1997







Earned 11 Varsity Letters in Field Hockey, Basketball, Track and Softball
4-time Mid Penn First Team All Star in Field Hockey
3-time Patriot News Big 11 selection in Field Hockey
First Team High School All American in Field Hockey her senior year
2 -time Mid Penn All Star in Basketball her sophomore and junior year
Led the Mid Penn Conference in scoring in basketball her junior year
3-time Mid Penn First Team All Star in Softball
Athletic Scholarship for Field Hockey to the University of Connecticut
Earned 4 Varsity Letters in Division One Field Hockey at UConn
4-time Big East Conference Champion in Field Hockey
Big East Conference Rookie of the Year in Field Hockey at UConn
4-time First Team Regional All American in Field Hockey at UConn
2-time National All America selection
Big East Conference Defensive Player of the Year
Big East Tournament MVP 







Jessica Totaro - Class of 1995






Earned 7 Varsity Letters in Cross Country and Track
4-time qualifier for the State Cross Country Meet
Led Cross Country team to 4 consecutive Mid Penn Titles and a 38-1 dual meet record
Led Cross Country team to 3 District Titles
Placed 3rd in PIAA Track and Field Championships in the 800 meters her senior year
Placed 7th in PIAA Track and Field Championships in the 1600 meters her junior year
Top point scorer on the Track and Field Team 3 consecutive years
Broke the Hershey record for the 800 and 1600 meters her junior and senior year
Mid Penn Conference Champion in the 800 and 1600 meters her junior year
District III Champion in the 800 and 1600 meters her senior year
Earned a 4 year Scholarship to Division One Mt. St. Mary's
Earned 12 Varsity Letters at Mt. St. Mary's for Cross Country, Indoor Track and Track and Field
Northeast Conference (NEC) Cross Country Champion her sophomore year







Joseph Viviano - Class of 1985






Earned 4 Varsity letters in Basketball and Golf
Scored 1,049 career points in Basketball
Member of 3 consecutive Mid Penn II Conference Championship teams from 1983-1985
Helped team to an impeccable 48-1 record in Mid Penn Conference play from 1983-1985
2 time selection to the Patriot News Big 15 Basketball team
McDonald's All-American Team Honorable Mention his senior year in Basketball
2-time PA All-State Basketball Team Honorable Mention
Member of the Central PA Sports Hall of Fame for Basketball
4 year starter at the College of the Holy Cross in Basketball
Made the 1986 All Rookie Team in the Metro Atlantic Athletic Conference for Basketball
Team Co- Captain his senior year at The College of the Holy Cross in Basketball
