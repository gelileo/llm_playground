Doug Arndt - Class of 1971






Won 3 Varsity letters in Basketball at Hershey
3-year starter in Basketball
Named UPI All State Honorable Mention in Basketball his senior year
Finished 2nd in scoring in the Capital Area Conference in Basketball his Junior and Senior year
Led the team in scoring and rebounding in Basketball his Junior and Senior year
2-time Capital Area Conference All-Star in Basketball
Team Captain his Senior year in Basketball

2-time Team Most Valuable Player in Basketball


Best Defensive Player his Junior year in Basketball


Most Improved Player his Junior year in Basketball


Hardest Worker Award his Junior year in Basketball


Played Basketball for 2 years at Fulton Montgomery Community College


Averaged 15.9 and 19.5 points per game while finishing as leading scorer in school history with 815 points


Was named the Fulton Montgomery Community College Athlete of the Year

Was a 2-time SUJCAC All-Star in Basketball
Was a 2-time MVCC All-Star in Basketball
Winner of the Coaches Award in Basketball
All Region MVP in Basketball
Led his team to a 35-11 record in 2 years in Basketball
Named to the All-Decade Team for the 1970s







Alex Barsanti - Class of 2006






Won 4 varsity letters in swimming at Hershey
2-time team captain in swimming
11-time Mid Penn Conference Champion in 4 different swimming events
15-time Mid Penn Conference All-Star Team in 6 different events in swimming
6-time District III Champion in 3 different events in swimming
6-time District III Medalist in 3 different events in swimming
4-time District III qualifier in 2 individual events in swimming
2-time PIAA Team State Champion in swimming
2-time PIAA State Champion in 2 events in swimming
8-time PIAA All State in swimming
2-time PIAA All State Honorable Mention in swimming
Attended Clemson University and then transferred to University of Tennessee
2010 SEC Champion in 200M medley relay in swimming at University of Tennessee
2010 SEC runner up in the 100M breaststroke in swimming at University of Tennessee
2010 All SEC first team in swimming at University of Tennessee
2010 NCAA runner up in the 200M medley relay in swimming at University of Tennessee
2010 NCAA 8th place finisher in the 100M breaststroke in swimming at University of Tennessee
2-time NCAA All American in swimming at the University of Tennessee
NCAA Academic All American at University of Tennessee
Team captain in swimming at University of Tennessee







Charles "Chuck" Ercole - Class of 1976






Won 6 varsity letters in football, wrestling and baseball
CAC Champions in football his junior year
2-time CAC Champions in baseball
Recipient of the "Pop" Kelchner Memorial Scholastic Athlete of the Year his senior year
Coaches Award for outstanding player in baseball his senior year
167 lb. sectional champion in wrestling his senior year
4 varsity letters in baseball at St. Joseph's University
3 time All East Coast Conference selection in baseball at St. Joseph's University
2-time team captain in baseball at St. Joseph's University
Career .335 batting average in baseball at St. Joseph's University
Inducted to the St. Joseph's University Hall of Fame for baseball in 2012







Greg Fastrich - Coach






He has been coaching at Hershey High School for 24 years

Women's Swimming and Diving

Won 12 District III Team Championships
35 of his women's relay teams have won District III Championships
48 of the women he has coached have won District III Championships
7 of his women's teams have won PIAA Team State Championships
23 of his women's relay teams have won PIAA State Championships
24 individual female swimmers have won PIAA State Championships

Men's Swimming and Diving

Won 15 District III Team Championships
32 of his men's relay teams have won District III Championships
67 individual male swimmers and divers have won District III Championships
6 of his men's teams have won PIAA State Championships
12 of his men's relay teams have won PIAA State Championships
23 individual male swimmers and divers have won PIAA State Championships
6-time PIAA Coach of the Year
Inducted into the PA Aquatics Hall of Fame in 2017







Larry Strait - Class of 1956/Coach






Won 3 varsity letters in tennis at Hershey
Won 2 varsity letters in tennis at Hershey Junior College
Won 2 varsity letters in Tennis at Lebanon Valley College
In 1957-58, he was the Northeast National Junior College Tennis Champion at Hershey Junior College
Head Coach of boys tennis for 32 years at Hershey
Head Coach of girls tennis for 17 years at Hershey
Combine record of 493-129 in tennis in Hershey
Produced 31 conference championships in 3 different leagues in tennis at Hershey
Produced 6 District III championships teams in Tennis
Coached the Boys Tennis Team to a PIAA State Championship in 1980
Coached 6 District III singles champions in tennis
Coached 3 District III doubles champions in tennis
Coached 3 PIAA State Champions in tennis
Coached 11 teams that were undefeated in tennis
Served on the District III Tennis Committee for over 20 years
Managed the capital area and the Mid Penn Tennis Conference until his retirement
