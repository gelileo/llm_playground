Dino Pellegrini - Class of 1959






Won 4 varsity letters in golf at Hershey High School
Went undefeated in golf all 4 years at Hershey High School
District III Champion in golf his junior year
Won the John Weitzel Sportsmanship Award in golf his Senior year
Won 4 varsity letters in swimming at Hershey High School
3-time team captain in swimming
Won 6 District III Bronze medals in swimming
Won 3 East Regional bronze medals in swimming
Won 1 East Regional silver medal in swimming his senior year
Won a PIAA silver medal in swimming his senior year
4-year member of the football team at Hershey High School







Jim Kirk - Class of 1986






Won 3 varsity letters in football at Hershey High School
Leading rusher and scorer in his junior year in football
District III Champions in football his junior year
Nominated for the Big 33 in his senior year in football
Won 2 varsity letters in basketball at Hershey High School
Won 3 varsity letters in track and field at Hershey High School
Led the team in individual points his senior year in track and field
3-time Mid-Penn team champion in Track and Field
District III champion in the discus his senior year in track and field
PIAA state runner-up in the shot put his senior year in track and field
Started 6 games at outside linebacker his junior year in football at the US Naval Academy
Won 3 varsity letters in football at the US Naval Academy
Graduate of the US Naval Academy in 1990
Spent 31 years as a Surface Warfare Officer, commanding 4 naval ships and selected as a 2 Star Admiral in the US Navy







Jim Martino - Class of 1974






Won 3 varsity letters in cross country at Hershey High School
District qualifier in cross country his junior and senior years
State qualifier in cross country his senior year
Won 3 varsity letters in track and field at Hershey High School
Leader of the undefeated track and field team his senior year
Member of the conference championship track and field team his senior year
District III Champion in the 330 intermediate hurdles in his senior year
Set both school and district records in the 330 intermediate hurdles in his senior year
4-year letter winner in track and field at Gettysburg College
Member of the Gettysburg College Athletic Hall of Fame
Named Middle Atlantic Conference Track Championship's Outstanding Athlete in 1978 as a member of Gettysburg's Track and Field team
Set the indoor school and conference record for the 440 relay and mile relay as a senior at Gettysburg College
5-time NCAA Division III All American in track and field
Trained for the 1980 Summer Olympics at the Olympic Training Camp in Scottsdale, Arizona







Michelle Sharp - Class of 1996






Won 4 varsity letters in swimming at Hershey High School
Won 4 straight Mid-Penn Conference Division Championships in swimming
1993 District III Team Champions in swimming
1993 PIAA AA Team Runner-up in swimming
1995 PIAA AA Team State Champions in swimming
3-time District III Team Championship Runner-up in swimming
2-time PIAA AA State Championship Team 3rd place in swimming
5-rime District III AA record holder in swimming
5-time PIAA AA state champion in swimming
3-time PIAA AA state record holder in swimming
4-time All American in swimming
Won 25 gold medals in swimming
Broke 9 individual school records in swimming
Member of the swimming team that broke 23 school records
Team Co-Captain her senior year in swimming
Awarded the Meritorious Achievement Award by the Central Chapter of the PA Sports Hall of Fame for swimming at Hershey in 1995
Awarded the Hershey Lions Club Scholar Athlete Award for swimming at Hershey her senior year
Was a member of the swimming team at Penn State University







Dennis "Beef" Tulli - Class of 1965






Won 3 varsity letters in football at Hershey High School
Won 3 varsity letters in baseball at Hershey High School
Lettered 4 years in football at Lebanon Valley College
Selected to all MAC in Football
Lettered 4 years in lacrosse at Lebanon Valley College
Selected to All Penn Del in Lacrosse
Started every game of football and lacrosse started for 4 years at Lebanon Valley College
Inducted into the Lebanon Valley College Hall of Fame in 1998
Inducted into the PA Sports Hall of Fame in 2017
Head football coach at Annville Cleona High School, Cedar Crest High School and Penn Manor High School from 1973-1986
Coach of the Year in the Lebanon-Lancaster League in 1974 and 1984
PIAA football official from 1987-1990
