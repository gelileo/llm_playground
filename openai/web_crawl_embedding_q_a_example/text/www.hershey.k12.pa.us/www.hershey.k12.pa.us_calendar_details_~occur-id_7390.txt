Event Details





Nov 6 2023


School Board Policy Committee Meeting



 5:00 PM

As a convenience for constituents, Derry Township School District Board of School Directors’ business meetings are made available via online livestream. In addition to being able to view the meeting in real time, online attendees also have the same opportunity to participate in public comment as those in-person.  Individual registration is required. The link can only be accessed by one user. If shared, the first to log in will be the only one to attend the meeting under that registration name. Those wishing to comment are asked to have their first, last name, and address listed on the registration. If not listed in this manner, the individual will not be recognized. Pre-registration will close five (5) minutes prior to the meeting. Any person registered by this time will be able to join the meeting at any time.

Pre-registration is required.

* Agendas are typically published the Friday evening prior to the meeting date
