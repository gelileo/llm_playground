Bob Watson, Sr. - Coach




Gary Garver - Class of 1984







Received 3 Varsity letters in football
Helped lead the team to an undefeated season in the South Penn Conference his Junior and Senior years in Football2 time Team Captain in Football
2 time selection to the County All Star Team that played against the City All Star to help support the U.S. War Bond
Lions Club Player of the Week his Senior year in Football, one of 14 to be so honored
Received 3 Varsity letters in Basketball
2-time Team Captain his Junior and Senior year in Basketball
Widely known as an outstanding Baseball player, however, Hershey High School didn’t field a Baseball Team during the years of the war
Spent 6 weeks in Spring Training with the Philadelphia Athletics







Camillo "Mimi" Gasper - Class of 1947











Nikki Smith Fisher - Class of 1988






The first female Athlete to win 12 Varsity letters for Field Hockey, Basketball and Softball
Team Captain in Field Hockey her Senior year
2-time Mid-Penn All-Star in Field Hockey her Junior and Senior year
Team Captain in Basketball her Senior year
2-time Mid-Penn All-Star in Basketball her Junior and Senior year
Set the Hershey single season assist record with 221 during the 1987-1988 season
Holds the career assist record at Hershey High School (703)
Patriot News Big 15 Honorable Mention in Basketball her Senior year
Picked to the Mid State Roundball Classic in Basketball her Senior year
Team Captain in Softball her Senior year
Mid-Penn All-Star Honorable Mention in Softball her Junior year
Mid-Penn All-Star Team in Softball her Senior year
Won 2 Varsity letters at Muhlenberg College in Basketball
2-Time Team leader in assists in Basketball at Muhlenberg College
Served as a coach in 3 sports at Hershey







Les Walters - Class of 1954











Bob Watson, Sr. - Coach







Coaching career at Hershey: 1966-2001
Coached in 5 decades
Assistant Wrestling Coach for 6 years
Head Wrestling Coach for 24 years
Coached 3 of Hershey’s State Champion Wrestlers
Assistant Football Coach for 32 years
Coached Football for 2 years at Junior High level
Coached Football for 30 years at High School level
PA Scholastic Football Coaches Association Assistant Coach of the Year Award in 1994
Coached Track & Field at the High School level for 35 years with16 years as Head Coach
Won 4 Mid-Penn Championships:  1988, 1993, 1995 and 1997
Coached 6 individual event State Champions
Coached 8 individual event District III Champions
Coached numerous District and State Medalists and qualifiers
