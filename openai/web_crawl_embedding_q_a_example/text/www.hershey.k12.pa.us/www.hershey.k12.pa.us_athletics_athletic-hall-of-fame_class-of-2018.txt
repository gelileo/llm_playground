Bob Sponaugle - Class of 1945






Won 9 varsity letters at Hershey in football, basketball and track and field
Team captain his senior year in football
Team captain his senior year in basketball
Selected to play in the City-County All Star Football Game, comparable to the Big 33 of that time
Won 4 varsity letters at University of Pennsylvania in football
2-way starter at defensive end and offensive tackle
Started 8 games at Penn as a freshman and played under Coach George Munger, the most storied Coach in Penn history.
Winning teams at Penn all 4 years with a record of 24-7-1
Played on Penn’s undefeated team in 1947
Selected to play in the North-South football game in 1949
Selected to play in the East-West Shriners Football Game in 1949
Played professional football with the NFL New York Bulldogs in 1949 (who then became the Baltimore and Indianapolis Colts)







David Mysel - Class of 1989






Won 3 varsity letters in basketball at Hershey
Mid Penn Keystone Division All Star in basketball his senior year at Hershey
Patriot News Big 15 basketball team his senior year at Hershey
Selected to play in the Roundball Classic in basketball his senior year at Hershey
Won 4 varsity letters in baseball at Hershey
Selected to the Patriot News Big 11 baseball team his senior year at Hershey
2 time Mid Penn Keystone Division Champions in baseball at Hershey
2-time selection to the Mid Penn Keystone Division All Star Team in baseball at Hershey
Selected to the All District baseball team his senior year at Hershey
Earned a full athletic scholarship to play baseball at the University of Maryland.
Won 3 Varsity letters in baseball at the University of Maryland
Winner of the University of Maryland Athletic Director Scholar Athlete Award
Taken in the 5th round by the Detroit Tigers in the 1992 Major League Baseball draft
Played in the Detroit Tigers System 4 ½ years, making the 40-man roster in 1994







Jeff Rosensteel - Coach






Head coach of the boys soccer team 2005 – 2011 at Hershey
Head coach of the girls soccer team 2006 – 2017 at Hershey
Won 2 District III Championships in soccer at Hershey
Won 6 Mid Penn Keystone Division Championships in soccer at Hershey
Won 3 Mid Penn Championships in soccer at Hershey
PIAA State Semi Finalists in 2009 in girls soccer at Hershey
PIAA State Champions in 2010 in girls soccer at Hershey
2 time MPC Keystone Division Coach of the Year
National Soccer Coaches Association of America Coach of the year in 2010
PA Soccer Coaches Association AAA Girls Coach of the Year in 2010
Career coaching record of 300 -101-13







Ken Kremer - Class of 1985







4 varsity letters in baseball at Hershey
4 year starting 1st baseman in baseball at Hershey
Member of the 1982 State Championship baseball team
2-time Mid Penn All Star Team in baseball at Hershey
Won 3 varsity letters in baseball at Rider University
Set 5 team records his sophomore season in baseball at Rider University
MVP of the East Coast Conference Championship Tournament during his sophomore year in baseball at Rider University
Named the NCAA Regional MVP after helping lead his team to the championship game against Georgia in his sophomore year in baseball at Rider University
.479 batting average won the NCAA Division One batting title his junior year in baseball at Rider University
2-time NCAA All American in baseball at Rider University
Drafted in the 4th round of the Major League Amateur Draft by the Milwaukee Brewers
Played 2 years in the Milwaukee Brewers System
Inducted into the Rider University Hall of Fame in 1998







Ken Whisler - Class of 1952






Won 7 varsity letters in football and wrestling at Hershey
District III Champion in wrestling his senior year at Hershey
Southeastern Regional Champion in wrestling his senior year at Hershey
PIAA State semi-finalist in wrestling his senior year at Hershey
Earned 2 varsity letters in wrestling at West Chester University
30-year career as head wrestling coach at Newport and Manheim Township High Schools
Coaching record of 331 – 115 in 30 years as a wrestling coach
10 sectional team titles
11 league championships
Coached 3 individual PIAA State Champions in wrestling
Coached 6 individual PIAA runners up in wrestling
Coached 22 individual District III Champions
Coached 1 National High School All American in wrestling
Coached 2 undefeated teams in wrestling
Member of the Pennsylvania Wrestling Hall of Fame
Member of the District III Wrestling Hall of Fame
Member of the Manheim Township Hall of Fame
3-time District III Wrestling Coach of the Year
6 time Lancaster-Lebanon League Wrestling Coach of the Year
