pauseplayWelcome to DTSDDerry Township School District is an award-winning public school system located in Hershey, Pennsylvania. Our community enjoys a legacy that began with founder, Milton S. Hershey. Our District is committed to excellence in education, offering a challenging curriculum that promotes critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity.
Learn more about us
With Trojan PrideWe serve approximately 3,200 students in kindergarten through twelfth grade residing in Derry Township, Dauphin County. We are proud to offer full day kindergarten.

Our AcademicsStudents know that when they are present in our buildings, they will have experiences that prepare them not just for the next assignment or test, but for the road of learning that will lead them to a full and satisfying life and career.

Our Mission & Vision




Our mission is to engage all students every day to help them achieve their greatest potential as global citizens and to cultivate an extraordinary learning environment in which all students and staff feel loved, welcomed and respected as they exceed academic expectations. 





Our Vision and Comprehensive Plan





Our District

We pride ourselves on our dedicated teachers, supportive staff, and involved parents and community members who work together to create a positive and inclusive learning environment for all students. Our district is home to five schools: Hershey Early Childhood Center, Hershey Primary Elementary School, Hershey Intermediate Elementary School, Hershey Middle School, and Hershey High School all of which provide students with a safe, nurturing, and stimulating learning environment.

			51 HHS Student Participate PMEA District 7 Band and Orchestra


51 DTSD musicians were part of close to 900 students auditioning for placement in the PMEA District 7 Band and PMEA District 7 Orchestra. District 7 is a strong district comprised of 7 counties in Central PA. We are thrilled to congratulate an incredible 30 students from DTSD who successfully qualified for these elite ensembles! Join us in congratulating our musicians.
