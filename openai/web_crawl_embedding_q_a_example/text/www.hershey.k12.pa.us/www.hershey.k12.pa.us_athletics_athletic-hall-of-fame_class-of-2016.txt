R. Edward Fornwalt - Class of 1945







3 time All South Penn as a tackle in football at Hershey
Finished 2nd in scoring in the South Penn Conference in basketball at Hershey
Was one of the originators of the Hershey High School track and field team. There was no team but he competed and represented Hershey
Was the first pole vaulter ever in track and field at Hershey 
Competed in District III in track and field as a pole vaulter and finished 5th his junior year at Hershey
Made the 1948 Associated Press All Pennsylvania State Teachers College Team
Made the 1949 Associated Press All Pennsylvania State Teachers College Second Team
Made the All-District Class B team from Indiana State Teachers College (W. Virginia, Ohio and Pennsylvania)
Named one of the greatest ends in the previous 25 years at Indiana State Teachers College by Athletic Director George P. Miller
Was the number 2 scorer for the All Pennsylvania State Teachers College Team
Hershey Assistant Football Coach in 1951
Hershey Head Football Coach from 1952-1954
Head Coach of the 1952 South Penn Champions and the 1953 South Penn Co-Champions
Hershey Assistant Basketball Coach from 1951-1954
Served as coach of the track and field team at Hershey







Carol Fricke - Coach







Head gymnastics coach for 30 years at Hershey
Coaching record of 141-124-1 with 5 undefeated seasons
Coached 9 District III Individual Champions
Coached 3 District III Team Championships
Coached numerous CAC/Mid Penn Individual and Team Championships
Coached 10 Individual State Champions
Coached 2 Team State Championships
Coached 2 Teams to 2nd place in State Championships







Roscoe Warner - Class Of 1927







Earned 11 varsity letters in football, basketball, baseball and track and field at Hershey High School
Helped lead the team to an 8-0-1 record, the first undefeated team in Hershey High School history
Helped lead the basketball team to a 22-2 record and Class B PIAA District III Title his senior year
Helped lead the basketball team to a 3rd consecutive Lower Dauphin County Title 
Excelled in football, basketball, and baseball at Shepherd University
Named Outstanding Offensive Lineman in WVIAC in 1934
Played Center on the basketball team at Shepherd University
Played outfield, first base and pitcher for the baseball team at Shepherd University
Head Football Coach at Lower Paxton High School for 4 years
Had a coaching record of 33-7-1 at Lower Paxton High School
Head Football Coach at Susquehanna High School from 1941-1959 with a record of 104-61-13
Coached his teams to 6 Lower Susquehanna Conference Championships in Football
Coached basketball at Susquehanna High School 1941-1948
Coached track and field at Susquehanna High School from 1948-1957
1976 inductee in the Central Chapter of the PA Sports Hall of Fame
In 1966 the Susquehanna High School Football Stadium was renamed after him, now called the Roscoe Warner Stadium 







Sarah Wilhite - Class Of 2005







Patriot News Big 11 First Team in field hockey her senior year at Hershey
2 time Patriot News All Underclassman in field hockey, her freshman and sophomore years at Hershey
2 time Mid Penn Conference First Team All Star in field hockey her junior and senior years at Hershey
3 Time Mid Penn Champions in field hockey at Hershey
4 time District III qualifier
2 time District III runner up
2 time District III 3rd Place finish
4 time PIAA State qualifier
PIAA Final 8 Her senior year in field hockey at Hershey
PIAA Final 4 her freshman year in field hockey at Hershey
2 Time Mid Penn Conference First Team All Star in soccer at Hershey
2 time Patriot News Big 11 First Team in soccer at Hershey
2 time Patriot News Big 11 All Underclassman Team in soccer at Hershey
Earned a Scholarship to play field hockey at the University of Michigan
NFHCA Division I All-Star Team her senior year at the University of Michigan
Big Ten First Team All-Star her senior year in field hockey at the University of Michigan
Team Captain her senior year in field hockey at the University of Michigan







Sherri Eyer - Class Of 1988







Scored 1056 points for her career in basketball at Hershey
A 2 time member of the Mid Penn II All Star Team in basketball at Hershey
Averaged 19 points and 12 rebounds per game her senior year in basketball at Hershey
Member of the Patriot News Big 15 in basketball her senior year at Hershey
Member of the East -West Roundball Classic All Star Team her senior year at Hershey
District and State qualifier her kunior year in cross country at Hershey
Placed 4th in the State in track and field in the 800M her sophomore year at Hershey
Placed 3rd in the State in track and field in the 300M hurdles her junior year at Hershey
District III Champion in track and field in the 300M hurdles her junior year
Received a full scholarship offer to Fairleigh Dickinson University for Basketball
Helped lead the basketball team to its first ever NCAA appearance at the University of Miami her Freshman year
Helped lead the basketball team to an appearance in the NIT Tournament her Sophomore year
Helped the basketball team to the New South Women’s Athletic Conference Championship her Junior year
In what was called the “dream season”, she helped her team go undefeated in the Big East, and win the Big East regular season and tournament titles
Over her 4 year career, she helped the basketball team to a 100-26 record
