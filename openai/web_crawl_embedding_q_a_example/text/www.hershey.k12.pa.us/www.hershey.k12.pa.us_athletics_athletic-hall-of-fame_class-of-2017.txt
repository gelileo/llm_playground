Allen Fasnacht - Class of 1946







Won 3 varsity letters in football at Hershey
He became the 4th member of his football team to be inducted into the Hall of Fame at Hershey
Won 4 varsity letters in wrestling at Hershey
Served as team captain in wrestling his senior year 
District III champion in wrestling his senior year
Regional champion in wrestling his senior year
PIAA runner up in wrestling his senior year
Hershey High Schools' first PIAA Place winner in wrestling his senior year
Won 2 varsity letters in golf at Hershey
Competed in basketball at Hershey Jr. College for 2 years
Competed in golf at Hershey Jr. College for 2 years
Wrestled at Penn State for 2 years
Assistant wrestling coach at Lebanon Valley College
Helped start the Hershey Jr. College wrestling program in 1964
Inducted into the District III Wrestling Hall of Fame in 1976
Inducted into the PIAA Wrestling Hall of Fame in 1977
Awarded the Lifetime Service to Wrestling Award by the National Wrestling Hall of Fame, PA Chapter in 2006
Officiated 468 PIAA/NCAA wrestling matches
Officiated 76 tournaments, including 8 NCAA National Championships







John Ricci - Class of 1979







Won 4 varsity letters in swimming and diving at Hershey
Placed 4th in District 3 for diving in his senior year
He set the pool record for 6 dives his senior year, a record that he held from 1979 – 2012
Won 3 varsity letters in track and field at Hershey
Ran the 400, 800, 1600 and relays for the track team
Set the record in the 400 meter run at 50.8, a record that held for 20 years
Won 2 varsity letters in cross country at Hershey
Set several course records home and away for cross country 
Ran 2 years of cross country at West Chester University
Ran 1 year of indoor track at West Chester University
On the swimming and diving team 2 years at West Chester University
Has coached the Hershey High School divers for over 33 years
Has coached the Milton Hershey School divers for over 10 years
Has coached 2 PIAA State Champions at diving
Has coached 2 PIAA State Championship runners up in Diving







Richard "Dick" Wright - Class of 1954







Won 3 varsity letters in football at Hershey
Played in the South Penn Conference Championship team in his junior and senior year
Played for Hall of Fame coaches Ed Fornwalt and Sterling Banta
Won 3 varsity letters in track and field at Hershey
Was a pole vault champion his senior year in track and field at Hershey
Set a school record for Pole Vault at 11’1¼” his senior year in Track and Field
Played freshman football at Franklin and Marshall College in 1954
Won 3 letters in football at Franklin and Marshall College
Was team co-captain his senior year in football at Franklin and Marshall College
Won 4 Varsity letters in track and field at Franklin and Marshall College
Was team co-captain his senior year in track and field at Franklin and Marshall College
Broke the Franklin and Marshall College pole vault record his senior year with a record-setting vault of 13’3¼”







Sarah Tarka Baer - Class of 2001







Won 4 varsity letters in soccer at Hershey
4 time Mid Penn 1st Team All Star in soccer at Hershey
2 time Patriot News Big 11 1st team selection in soccer at Hershey
3 time team captain in soccer at Hershey
Patriot News Big 11 Top Underclassman in her sophomore year in soccer
Mid Penn Senior All Star Team in soccer her senior year
Colonial Division Player of the Year her senior year in soccer
2 time MVP Defensive Player Award in soccer
Mid Penn Conference Player of the Year her senior year in soccer
PA. Soccer Coaches Association 1st Team All State selection her senior year in soccer
NSCAA Region II All America Team in soccer her senior year
Won 2 varsity letters in field hockey at Hershey
Mid Penn 2nd Team All Star her junior year in field hockey
Mid Penn Capital Division Champions her junior year in field hockey
Won 2 Varsity letters at the University of Rochester in Soccer
University Athletic Association Rookie of the Year her freshman year in soccer at University of Rochester
University Athletic Association 1st Team All Star her freshman year in soccer at University of Rochester
University Athletic Association All Academic Team her sophomore and junior year







Tom Sotack - Class of 1976






Won 3 varsity letters in football at Hershey
Was named MVP of the Cocoa Bean Game his sophomore year
All CAC in football his sophomore year
Named to Mid-State Top 100 in football his senior year
Football team captain his senior year
Won 2 varsity letters in basketball at Hershey
Member of the District 3 Championship team in basketball his sophomore year
Member of the CAC Championship team in track and field at Hershey
Was a medalist in the District 3 4x400 relay team
Helped lead his baseball team to 2 consecutive CAC Championships
All CAC in baseball in his junior and senior years
Team captain in baseball his senior year
Earned a baseball scholarship to the University of South Carolina
Played baseball 2 years at University of South Carolina. Team played in the College World Series in 1977
Played 1 year at DeKalb South Junior College where he made All Georgia JUCO Team and All Regional JUCO Tournament Team
Finished at Shippensburg University where he helped his team make it to Regional Division II playoffs 2 consecutive years
