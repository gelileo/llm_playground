John Hetrick - Class of 1965







10 HHS Varsity Letters including 4 in Baseball, 3 in Football and 3 in Basketball
Drafted by Boston Red Sox in 1965 - chose not to play at the time
First HHS player to play in Pennsylvania Big 33 Football Game
Scholarship to University of Maryland - Lettered 2 years in Baseball; 1 year in Football
All-ACC in Football; All-American in Baseball
Drafted by Detroit Tigers and played 6 years in the system 







John Hummer - Class of 1933






Earned a record 13 HHS Varsity letters
Football - 1930, 1031, 1932, 1933
Basketball - 1930, 1931, 1932, 1933
Baseball - 1930, 1931, 1932, 1933
Track & Field - 1932
Member and running back of 1933 Football Team which achieved HHS all-time best record to date
President of HHS Varsity H Club, President of HHS Athletic Club
Widely acclaimed in publications of the 1930s as the HHS best athlete ever
Specially cited by HHS faculty to students as a role model into the 1960s







Hank Kuzma - Class of 1974







Won 7 Varsity letters in Basketball, Cross Country and Track & Field
2-time Patriot News Big 15 selection in Basketball - 1972-73, 1973-74
Basketball Team Captain - 1973-74
CAC Champions - 1972-73
District 3 Champions - 1973-74
Pennsylvania All-State Team - 1974
Pennsylvania Dapper Dan Round Ball Classic - 1974
At the time held HHS career scoring and rebounding records
Appointment to US Naval Academy - 1974
4 Varsity letters in basketball - 1974-75, 1975-76, 1976-77, 1977-78
Basketball Team Captain - 1977-78
At the time held Naval Academy records for career scoring, rebounding and field goal percentage
Awarded NAAA Sword as top athlete during his 4 years at the Academy
Inducted into Central PA Sports Hall of Fame 







Don McCorkel - Class of 1973







Won 8 Varsity letters in Football, Wrestling and Track & Field
District 3 Champion in Javelin - 1973
2 time Sectional Wrestling Champion in 1971-72 and 1972-73
District 3 and Regional Champion 1973
First State Wrestling Champion at Hershey High School in 1973
Won 4 Varsity letters in Wrestling at Lehigh University- 1973-74, 1974-75, 1975-76, 1976-1977
3 time E.I.W.A Champion:  1974-75, 1975-96, 1976-77
Finished 3rd in NCAA Tournament in 1977







Carol Backenstose Miller - Class of 1971






Earned 4 Varsity Letters in Tennis - 1968, 1969, 1970, 1971
Compiled a HHS career record of 43-1 in tennis matches while completing in the first position
Won 100 matches in Central Penn and Middle States tennis tournaments
Earned 4 Varsity letters in Tennis at Penn State University while competing in the first position attaining a record of 22-6
Attained a Singles ranking of #3 in the Middle States Tennis Assn. And a national #51 ranking in the United States Lawn Tennis Association, 1971
Attained a national #10 Doubles ranking in the United States Lawn Tennis Association, 1971
Selected as a member of the Penn State Sports Museum
Selected as a member of the Central Penn Chapter of the PA Sports Hall of Fame, inducted in 1990







Chris Villarrial - Class of 1991







Won 3 HHS Varsity Letters in Football - 1988, 1989, 1990
2 time Mid Penn All Star - 1989, 1990
Team Captain and MVP - 1990
Patriot News PA Big 33 Football Team - 1991 
Won 2 Varsity Letters in Wrestling - 1988-89, 1989-90
Won 3 Varsity Letters in Track & Field - 1989, 1990, 1991
4 Varsity Letters in Football at Indiana University of PA - 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995
2 time All PSAC and 2 time All American - 1994, 1995
Jersey retired in 2000 from Indiana University of PA
Professional Football Player in the NFL







Bob Watson, Jr. - Class of 1980







Won 3 HHS Varsity Letters in Football - 1977, 1978, 1979
Team co-captain in Football - 1979
Patriot News PA Big 33 Football Team - 1980
Member of 1st Team All CAC, Pennsylvania Blue Chip and All State Teams - 1979
Won 3 HHS Varsity Letters in Track & Field - 1978, 1979, 1980
4-time District 3 Champion in Shot Put and Discus - 1979, 1980
State Champion in Shot Put - 1980
Won 4 Varsity Letters in Football and Track & Field at Virginia Tech - 1980 to 1984 
2 Time Indoor Track and Field State Champion in Shot Put 1979, 1980
