			Solar Eclipse (April 8) Early Dismissal



Mar 21 2024



Derry Township School District has made the decision to dismiss school early on April 8, 2024 due to the solar eclipse. Due to the timing of the eclipse coinciding with student dismissal at our Primary and Secondary buildings, we will dismiss all students at the times listed below as a precautionary measure for student safety. This day will be an early dismissal regardless of the weather conditions (sunny, cloudy). See headline for more details and dismissal times.

			2024-2025 Kindergarten Registration Mailing List



Dec 8 2023



Derry Township School District is underway with the planning for our incoming Kindergarteners for the 2024-2025 school year! A child is eligible for admission to kindergarten if s/he has attained the age of five (5) years before the first day of September. Sign up for our mailing list to stay informed on upcoming mailings and events for our 2024-2025 Kindergarten class! The first event will be our Parent Information Night which will be Wednesday, January 31at 6:00 PM on Zoom.

Dec 5 2023



51 DTSD musicians were part of close to 900 students auditioning for placement in the PMEA District 7 Band and PMEA District 7 Orchestra. District 7 is a strong district comprised of 7 counties in Central PA. We are thrilled to congratulate an incredible 30 students from DTSD who successfully qualified for these elite ensembles! Join us in congratulating our musicians.
