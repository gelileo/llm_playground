Chris Bickel - Class of 1992






Won 4 Varsity letters in Football at Hershey High School
Won 4 Varsity letters in Track and Field at Hershey High School
Won 1 Varsity letter in Basketball at Hershey High School
Started 44 consecutive games in Football in 4 years
2-time team Captain in Football
2-time MVP of the Cocoa Bean game vs. Milton Hershey
2-time Mid Penn Division II All Star at Quarterback and Safety
Led his team to the PIAA District III playoffs in his junior year
2-time Mid Penn Division II All Star in the hurdles in Track and Field
Placed 9th in the PIAA State Championship 110 meter hurdles his senior year
4-year starter at Safety at Shippensburg University
1st Team All PSAC at Safety his senior year at Shippensburg University
2nd Team Academic All-American his senior year at Shippensburg University







Kathy Krall - Class of 1988






Won 10 Varsity letters in 3 sports at Hershey High School
Won 4 Varsity letters in Track and Field
Won 3 Varsity letters in Field Hockey
Won 3 Varsity letters in Basketball
3-time winner of the Tri-Athlete Award at Hershey High School
Currently holds the Hershey High School Shot Put record of 40 feet, 10 inches
Mid Penn Champion in Shot Put her freshman year
PIAA District III AAA Champion in Shot Put her freshman year
PIAA State Qualifier in Shot Put her freshman year
Mid-Penn Champion in Shot Put her sophomore year
Finished 2nd in District III AA Shot Put her sophomore year
Mid-Penn Champion in Javelin her junior year
Finished 3rd in District III AA Shot Put her junior year
Finished 3rd in the PIAA State Championship in Discus her junior year
Finished 4th in the PIAA State Championship in Shot Put her junior year
Mid Penn Champion in Shot Put her senior year
Finished 4th in both the District III & PIAA State Championships in Shot Put her senior year
Mid Penn Division II All-Star Team as a Field Hockey Goalie her senior year
Threw Shot Put and Discus for 2 years at East Stroudsburg University
1989 Medalist in Shot Put her freshman year at East Stroudsburg University
Has coached throwing events for the men’s and women’s Track and Field teams at Spring Grove Area High School for 16 years
Has coached 7 York-Adams League champions, 6 District medalists and 2 State medalists in throwing events at Spring Grove Area High School







David Nolan - Class of 1988






Won 4 Varsity letters in Swimming at Hershey High School
Competed in Freestyle, Backstroke, Breaststroke, IM and Relay Events
15-time PIAA District 3 Champion in 7 events
12-time PIAA State Champion in 5 events
Holds the Hershey High School team record in 10 events his senior year
Held 5 National High School Swimming records his senior year
High School All-American
3-time Pennsylvania Swimmer of the Year
John Travers Scholar Athlete Award
Earned a full scholarship to Stanford University
Set 3 team records in swimming at Stanford University
Won 9 conference titles at Stanford University
17-time All-American at Stanford University
2011 US National Team member
2012 US Olympic Trials
2016 US Olympic Trials: finished 3rd in the 200 IM behind Michael Phelps and Ryan Lochte







Katie Nolan McComb - Class of 1994






Won 2 Varsity letters in Tennis at Hershey High School
Won 4 Varsity letters in Swimming at Hershey High School
Competed in Freestyle, Butterfly, Backstroke and Relay Events
10-time PIAA District 3 Champion in 4 events
11-time PIAA State Champion in 4 events
Holds the Hershey High School team record for the 100 Butterfly her senior year
Held records in 10 events in her senior year at Hershey High School
2008-2009 Scholastic All-American honor from US Swimming
PIAA District III Academic Excellence/Sportsmanship Award
Pop Kelchner award winner
Earned a full scholarship to the University of North Carolina
5th place in 100 Butterfly at 2013 NCAA Championships
Current record-holder in 3 events at the University of North Carolina
Honorable Mention All-American
NCAA All-American
Placed 14th in the 200 Butterfly in the 2012 Olympic Trials
Placed 12th in the 200 Butterfly in the 2012 US Open
Placed 14th in the 100 Butterfly in the 2012 US Open







Laurie Sprecher - Class of 1994






Won 4 Varsity letters in Basketball at Hershey High School
Won 2 Varsity letters in Softball at Hershey High School
Scored 1,271 career points in Basketball making her the All-Time leading scorer at Hershey High School
Led her team to 2 consecutive Mid Penn Division II Championships in Basketball
Led her team to 2 District III Runner-up finishes in Basketball
Finished 4th in scoring in the Mid Penn Division II conference her sophomore year with 12 points, 3 assists and 2 steals per game
Patriot News 1st Team All-Underclassman her sophomore year in Basketball
Finished 2nd in scoring in the Mid Penn Division II conference her junior and senior years in Basketball
Averaged 17 points per game and made 24 3-point shots her senior year in Basketball
3-time Mid Penn Division II All-Star in Basketball
2–time member of the Big 15 in Basketball
Played 1 year of Basketball at Bloomsburg University
