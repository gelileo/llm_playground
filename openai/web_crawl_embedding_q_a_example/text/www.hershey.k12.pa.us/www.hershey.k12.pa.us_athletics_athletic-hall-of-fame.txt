Athletic Hall of Fame




The Derry Township School District created the Hershey High School Athletic Hall of Fame in the fall of 2004 to recognize individuals for significant career accomplishments in the field of athletics. The Hall of Fame serves the dual purpose of recognizing such accomplishments and providing role models for current students in the Derry Township School District. The first induction of the newly created Hall of Fame was held on Friday, October 14, 2005
Athletic HOF Bylaws 




Nomination Procedure


Each spring, the Hall-of-Fame committee solicits nominations for induction into the Hershey High School Hall-of-Fame.  To qualify for recognition, nominees must meet the following criteria:


The nominee must have graduated at least 10 years prior to the year of nomination.


The nominee must have been an athlete, coach, or individual who achieved noteworthy and outstanding accomplishments for the athletic program of the Derry Township School District.


The nominee may be recognized posthumously.
The Hershey High School Athletic Hall of Fame is currently accepting nominations. All nominations should be sent to the High School Athletic Office or directly to John Christopher at jcchristopher45@gmail.com. Any nominations received will be in consideration for 2022 and beyond.
If you have any questions regarding nominations, please contact John Christopher, jcchristopher45@gmail.com or 717-421-1395.

PDF Nomination Form

Nominations forms can also be picked up at the Hershey High School Athletic Office.
